Approve only when QC, sizing, and parcel logic are clear.

Hold

Pause for a missing close-up or measurement.

Reroute

Exchange when version, color, or detail does not match.

Return

Reject if the intake note no longer supports shipping.

Buyer experience note

How this HubBuyCN Spreadsheet reviews a find before parcel-bay release

This HubBuyCN Spreadsheet is organized for shoppers who want more than a fast link list. Each route dock is written around practical buying evidence: listing photos, seller consistency, warehouse QC images, sizing proof, packaging choices, and parcel weight. The goal is to help a buyer know what to check before approving international shipping.

The sorting-hub method is simple: shortlist more than one candidate, compare visible details, hold the item if QC intake photos are unclear, ask for size scans when fit matters, and review the full parcel bay before paying for the final shipping line. That process supports buyer confidence because every recommendation is connected to an observable check.

These pages are independent research notes for 2026. They are not official HubBuyCN pages and cannot guarantee seller behavior, availability, price, or shipping performance. Use the intake notes as a decision checklist, then verify the current item page, size chart, QC photos, and parcel terms before ordering.
